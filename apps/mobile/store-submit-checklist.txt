Pre-submit checklist (manual in Apple App Store Connect + Google Play Console)

Apple (ios.buildNumber bumps each upload)
- Bundle ID: com.chineselearning.app matches Xcode/EAS credentials.
- App Privacy questionnaire aligned with API/profile/auth/microphone/speech data use.
- Support URL + Privacy Policy URL (public HTTPS).
- Screenshots per required device sizes; age rating; export compliance (ITSAppUsesNonExemptEncryption false).
- Device smoke test: login, lessons, speech/microphone.

Google Play (android.versionCode bumps each upload)
- Package name: com.chineselearning.app matches Play app ID.
- Upload AAB from: pnpm eas:build:android (repo root) or apps/mobile eas:build:android.
- Data safety form; content rating; target audience; privacy policy URL.
- Internal/open testing before production rollout.

Repo scripts (from monorepo root)
- pnpm mobile:doctor — expo-doctor
- pnpm eas:build:ios / pnpm eas:build:android — production store binaries

Device smoke tests (release builds)
- Fresh install: onboarding → login → complete one lesson → speech exercise accepts mic permission.
